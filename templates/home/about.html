About This To-Do App

This simple yet powerful To-Do List application helps you organize your daily tasks efficiently. Stay focused, prioritize your work, and never miss a deadline!

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and delete your tasks with ease.
  • Mark tasks as complete to keep track of progress.
  • Filter tasks by status (all, completed, pending).
  • Responsive design so you can manage your tasks on any device.

How It Works

Simply enter your task in the input field, then add it to your list. You can update the task details or remove tasks you no longer need. The app saves your progress so you can pick up right where you left off.
